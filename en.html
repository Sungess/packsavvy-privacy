1. Privacy Policy Overview

PackSavvy ("we", "our", "the app") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the PackSavvy mobile application.

Core Principle: PackSavvy stores all data locally on your device and does not transmit personal information to external servers.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Trip Information: Trip names, destinations, dates, and purposes
  • Checklist Data: Packing lists, completion status, bag information
  • App Settings: Language preferences, theme settings
  • Travel Documents: Images or PDF files of passports, visas, flight tickets, etc.
  • Document Images: Document files captured by camera or uploaded

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Operating system version, app version
  • Usage Patterns: Feature usage frequency (processed locally only)

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Creating and managing travel packing checklists
  • Safe storage and management of travel documents
  • Providing offline document access services
  • Providing personalized app settings
  • Improving and optimizing app functionality
  • Resolving technical issues and providing customer support

4. Information Storage and Security

Local Storage: All personal information is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to external servers or cloud services.

4.1 Security Measures

  • Encrypted local storage within the device
  • All document files are encrypted and stored inside the device
  • Document data is not transmitted to servers and external access is blocked
  • Complete data and document deletion when the app is uninstalled
  • No external network transmission

4.2 Data Retention

Data and documents are retained on your device until you delete the app or manually delete the data.

5. Information Sharing

No Information Sharing: PackSavvy does not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Information may only be disclosed in the following exceptional circumstances:

  • When required by law or court order
  • With your explicit consent
  • To address security threats to the app

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Rectification: Modify trip information and checklists at any time
  • Erasure: Delete individual data or all data by uninstalling the app
  • Portability: Export data functionality when needed

7. Children's Privacy

PackSavvy does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will immediately delete such information.

8. International Data Transfers

Since PackSavvy uses only local storage, no cross-border data transfers occur.

9. Policy Changes

When this Privacy Policy is updated:

  • Users will be notified through app updates
  • Significant changes will be separately announced
  • Updated policy takes effect 7 days after notification
